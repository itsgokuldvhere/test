New Arrivals

To Kill a Mockingbird

A powerful novel set in the American South during the 1930s, focusing on themes of racial injustice and moral growth. The story is told through the eyes of Scout Finch, whose father, Atticus Finch, is a lawyer defending a black man wrongly accused of raping a white woman.

1984

A dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society under constant surveillance by Big Brother. The protagonist, Winston Smith, works for the Party rewriting history but secretly yearns for freedom and truth, leading to his rebellion against the oppressive regime.


Pride and Prejudice

A classic romantic novel that explores the issues of marriage, class, and morality in early 19th-century England. The story centers on Elizabeth Bennet, a sharp-witted young woman, and her evolving relationship with the wealthy but aloof Mr. Darcy.


One Hundred Years of Solitude

A landmark of magical realism, this novel chronicles the multi-generational story of the Buend√≠a family in the fictional town of Macondo. Themes of love, power, and destiny are woven into a rich tapestry of fantastical events and vibrant characters.

The Great Gatsby

Set in the Jazz Age of the 1920s, this novel explores themes of decadence, idealism, and the American Dream. The mysterious and wealthy Jay Gatsby throws extravagant parties in hopes of reuniting with his lost love, Daisy Buchanan, leading to tragic consequences.

Atomic Habits

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear is a guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones through small, consistent changes. It offers practical strategies backed by scientific research, daily actions can lead to significant, long-term improvements in personal and professional life.

Crime and Punishment

A psychological thriller set in St. Petersburg, Russia, this novel tells the story of Raskolnikov, a former student who commits a murder and then struggles with guilt and paranoia. The book delves deeply into the human psyche and explores themes of morality, redemption, and the nature of evil.

The Alchemist

This modern classic follows Santiago, a young shepherd in search of a worldly treasure located in the Egyptian pyramids. Along his journey, he learns profound lessons about following his dreams, the nature of true wealth, and listening to his heart, encapsulating themes of spirituality and self-discovery.
